The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has announced the inclusion of “Ahmednagar Merchant’s Co-op. Bank Ltd., Ahmednagar” in the Second Schedule of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934. This inclusion was notified through CO.DOR.RAUG.No.S2017/08.02.001/2025-26 on June 12, 2025, and was published in the Gazette of India on July 8, 2025.
